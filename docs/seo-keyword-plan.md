# Instagram Downloader SEO Keyword Plan

## Method used

1. Google autocomplete sampling (US locale): queried Instagram downloader intent phrases and collected suggested expansions.
2. External keyword benchmark review: checked a public Instagram keyword list from Keysearch.
3. Mapped only public-link download intent terms to avoid irrelevant traffic.

## Priority keyword clusters

### Core commercial intent
- instagram downloader
- insta downloader
- ig downloader
- instadownloader
- instagram video downloader

### Post-link intent
- instagram post downloader
- download instagram post
- download instagram post link
- instagram post link downloader

### Reel-link intent
- instagram reel downloader
- download instagram reel
- download instagram reel link
- save instagram reel

### Media-type intent
- instagram photo downloader
- instagram image downloader
- instagram carousel downloader
- instagram link downloader

## Notes

- Some autocomplete terms (for example, profile-picture-only and extension-specific queries) were excluded where they did not match the product flow.
- Current optimization stays aligned with public-link support to avoid misleading search intent.
